Nexus Band Features

  • Comfortable and child-safe material
  • Embedded unique QR code linking to child’s essential information
  • Quick access to parental contact details in case of emergency
  • Water-resistant and durable for everyday use
  • No app required; scan with any smartphone camera

Our Nexus Band ensures that parents can always stay connected to their children’s safety, whether at school, during trips, or outdoor activities.
